YCB Certification Programs

YCB 200-Hour Certification

Description: A foundational course designed for beginners interested in becoming yoga instructors. It covers basic yoga postures, breathing techniques, meditation, and yoga philosophy.

  • Key Learning Areas:
    • Fundamental asanas (postures)
    • Pranayama (breath control)
    • Introduction to yoga philosophy and anatomy
    • Basics of teaching methodology
  • Eligibility: No prior experience in yoga required.
  • Outcome: Certification as a Level 1 Protocol Instructor, enabling graduates to lead beginner-level classes.

YCB 400-Hour Certification

Description: An intermediate program that builds on the foundational 200-hour training. It includes more advanced practices and deeper knowledge of yoga philosophy.

  • Key Learning Areas:
    • Advanced asanas and sequencing
    • Detailed anatomy and physiology
    • Adjustments and teaching advanced classes
    • Deeper study of yoga texts like the Bhagavad Gita and Yoga Sutras
  • Eligibility: Completion of a 200-hour yoga certification.
  • Outcome: Certification as a Level 2 Wellness Instructor, preparing instructors to teach intermediate-level classes.

YCB 800-Hour Certification

Description: An advanced certification that combines the 200-hour and 300-hour programs into one comprehensive course, focusing on mastering yoga practices and teaching skills.

  • Key Learning Areas:
    • All aspects of 200- and 400-hour training
    • Advanced techniques in yoga therapy and holistic wellness
    • Hands-on teaching experience and mentorship
    • Research and case studies in yoga practice
  • Eligibility: Open to beginners or graduates of the 200-hour certification.
  • Outcome: Certification as a Level 3 Teacher and Evaluator, enabling graduates to conduct both beginner and advanced classes.

YCB 1600-Hour Certification

Description: A specialized training program focusing on advanced yoga therapy techniques for physical and mental health issues.

  • Key Learning Areas:
    • Yoga therapy for lifestyle disorders (diabetes, hypertension, etc.)
    • Tailored yoga sequences for mental health (anxiety, depression)
    • In-depth study of yoga therapy techniques
  • Eligibility: Completion of a 800-hour certification.
  • Outcome: Certification as an Assistant Yoga Therapist, capable of guiding therapeutic yoga sessions.

YCB 200-Hour Certification

Description: The highest level of yoga instructor training, focusing on mastery in therapeutic yoga practices and advanced teaching skills.

  • Key Learning Areas:
    • Yoga therapy for chronic diseases and advanced ailments
    • Developing therapeutic programs for groups and individuals
    • Mastery in advanced yoga postures and techniques
    • Research methodologies in yoga therapy
  • Eligibility: Completion of a 1600-hour certification or equivalent experience.
  • Outcome: Certification as a Level 5 Yoga Therapist, qualifying graduates to work as experts in therapeutic yoga.
