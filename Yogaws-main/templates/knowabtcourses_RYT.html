RYT Certification Programs

RYT 200-Hour Certification

Description: A foundational course for aspiring yoga instructors covering fundamental yoga principles, teaching techniques, and practical training.

  • Key Learning Areas:
    • Basic and intermediate yoga asanas (postures)
    • Pranayama (breathing techniques) and meditation
    • Yoga philosophy, anatomy, and ethics
    • Teaching methodology and class sequencing
  • Eligibility: No prior teaching experience required; open to yoga enthusiasts.
  • Outcome: Certification as a RYT 200, allowing graduates to teach beginner to intermediate-level yoga classes.

RYT 300-Hour Certification

Description: An advanced course designed to deepen the knowledge and teaching skills acquired in the RYT 200 program, focusing on alignment, adjustments, and specialized practices.

  • Key Learning Areas:
    • Advanced asanas and modifications
    • Deeper study of pranayama and meditation techniques
    • Yoga therapy and injury prevention
    • Teaching strategies for diverse populations
  • Eligibility: Completion of RYT 200 certification.
  • Outcome: Certification as a RYT 500 (when combined with an RYT 200), allowing graduates to offer more specialized and advanced classes.

RYT 500-Hour Certification

Description: A comprehensive yoga teacher training program integrating both RYT 200 and RYT 300 curricula, providing in-depth expertise in yoga instruction and practice.

  • Key Learning Areas:
    • Mastery of asanas, adjustments, and props
    • Therapeutic yoga and advanced teaching methodologies
    • Philosophy, Ayurveda, and subtle body studies
    • Business and ethics of yoga teaching
  • Eligibility: Open to both beginners and those with prior training (if completing in one program) or graduates of RYT 200.
  • Outcome: Certification as a RYT 500, qualifying instructors to teach at all levels and conduct workshops or teacher training programs.

RYT 700-Hour Certification

Description: A specialized training program focusing on advanced yoga therapy techniques for physical and mental health issues.

  • Key Learning Areas:
    • Yoga therapy for lifestyle disorders (diabetes, hypertension, etc.)
    • Tailored yoga sequences for mental health (anxiety, depression)
    • In-depth study of yoga therapy techniques
  • Eligibility: Completion of a 500-hour certification.
  • Outcome: Certification as an Assistant Yoga Therapist, capable of guiding therapeutic yoga sessions.

RYT 900-Hour Certification

Description: The highest level of RYT certification, emphasizing deep specialization, research, and yoga therapy, preparing instructors to become yoga educators, mentors, and program developers.

  • Key Learning Areas:
    • Personalized therapeutic yoga instruction and research-based approaches
    • Integration of Ayurveda, modern science, and yoga therapy
    • Mentorship and leadership in yoga teacher training
    • Business development and yoga education strategies
  • Eligibility: Completion of RYT 700 certification.
  • Outcome: Certification as a RYT 900, positioning graduates as senior yoga instructors, master trainers, and yoga education specialists.
